June 2026 is shaping up to be one of the most event-dense months in the Indian tech calendar. Mumbai is headlined by SAP NOW AI Tour (June 12), SAS Innovate on Tour (June 3), a Global AI Mumbai community day, and an active AI Tinkerers chapter. Bengaluru is dominated by the inaugural AgentCon (June 20), recurring AI Tinkerers meetups, Agentic AI Community events, and multiple AICamp & Google Developer Group sessions. A Gartner summit lands in Mumbai in September (not June), and several Gartner events are confirmed for June 1–2 in Mumbai. The data science, GenAI, and agentic AI themes dominate across both cities this month. Many of the best events are free.
Key insight: The most impactful confirmed June event in Mumbai is the SAP NOW AI Tour (June 12, Jio World Convention Centre) and SAS Innovate on Tour (June 3). In Bengaluru, AgentCon (June 20) is the marquee event — a full-day free conference by Global AI Community on AI agents. Both cities have active recurring community meetups (AI Tinkerers, AICamp, Global AI) happening throughout the month. Gartner's flagship D&A summit is confirmed for Mumbai on June 1–2, making the first week of June particularly powerful for data professionals.
